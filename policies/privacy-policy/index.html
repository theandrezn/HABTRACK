Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how HabTrack collects, uses, and shares information when you visit our website, interact with our advertising, or purchase our digital products.

Information we collect

Depending on how you use the website, we may collect:

  • contact and order information, such as your name, email address, and transaction details;
  • technical information, such as device type, browser, IP address, pages viewed, and approximate location;
  • marketing attribution information, such as UTM parameters, campaign identifiers, referral URLs, and advertising click identifiers;
  • communications you send to our support team.

How we use information

We use information to process purchases, provide product access, respond to support requests, prevent fraud, improve our website, understand campaign performance, and deliver or measure advertising.

Cookies, pixels, and analytics

We may use cookies, pixels, and similar technologies. Our website uses UTMify for attribution and tracking. UTMify may process campaign data and interact with advertising platforms such as Meta (Facebook and Instagram) and Google to help measure conversions and improve advertising performance.

Service providers

We may share information with service providers that help us operate the website, process payments, deliver digital products, provide customer support, analyze performance, and measure advertising. These providers may process information under their own privacy policies.

Data retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, including support, accounting, legal compliance, fraud prevention, and dispute resolution.

Your choices and rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of certain personal information. You may also limit cookies through your browser settings. Some website functions and attribution measurements may be affected when cookies are disabled.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when our services or legal obligations change. The date at the top of this page identifies the latest version.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact support@habtrack.com.
